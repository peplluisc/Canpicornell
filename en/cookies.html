Cookies Policy

This website uses cookies to improve your user experience and guarantee the correct technical functioning of direct bookings.

1. What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your browser when you visit it. It allows remembering information about your navigation, technical configuration preferences, or basic interactions.

2. Cookies Used on this Site

Our website only uses technical cookies necessary for operation and safety:

  • Technical Session Cookies (First-party): Used to maintain language preference consistency and manage CSRF security tokens during bookings to prevent external cross-site request attacks.
  • Technical Payment Cookies (Third-party - Stripe): When interacting with Stripe checkout, technical cookies are set strictly to process secure payments and detect/prevent online fraudulent activity.

Please note: We do not use advertising cookies, intrusive tracking cookies, or third-party behavioral analytics cookies.

3. Disabling Cookies

You can restrict, block, or delete cookies from this or any other website by adjusting your web browser settings:

  • Cookies settings in Google Chrome
  • Cookies settings in Apple Safari
  • Cookies settings in Mozilla Firefox
  • Cookies settings in Microsoft Edge
