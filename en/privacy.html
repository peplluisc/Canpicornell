Privacy Policy

At Can Picornell, we care about the protection of your personal data. Below, we detail how we process the information you provide us through this website.

1. Data Controller

The controller of the data collected is the owner of the Can Picornell holiday villa. If you wish to contact us for any data protection queries, you can do so by sending an email to: info@canpicornell.com.

2. Purposes of Data Processing

We process your personal data for the following specific purposes:

  • To manage your direct booking requests submitted via the availability and contact form.
  • To facilitate the secure deposit and booking payment process via Stripe.
  • To comply with Spanish legal obligations applicable to tourist accommodations, including mandatory traveler registration with competent police authorities.
  • If you checked the consent box, to send you newsletters or special holiday villa discount offers.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

The legal basis for processing your data is:

  • The execution of a contract or pre-contract in the case of direct booking requests.
  • The explicit consent given by the user when submitting the form with the mandatory check boxes ticked.
  • Compliance with a legal obligation (official guest registration with police).

4. Data Recipients

Your personal data will not be sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except with:

  • Spanish state security forces and bodies (Police / Guardia Civil) in compliance with Spanish regulations on registering travelers in tourist accommodation.
  • The secure payment provider (Stripe) to process transaction charges relating to the reservation.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, limit, or delete your data at any time. To exercise these rights, simply send an email explaining your request to info@canpicornell.com, providing appropriate proof of identity.
