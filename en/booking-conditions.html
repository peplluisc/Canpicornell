Booking & Cancellation Conditions

These conditions regulate the direct booking request process and guest stays at the Can Picornell holiday villa (official tourist license ETV/3819).

1. Request and Payment Process

  • Request: By filling out the booking form, you place a booking request that will be reviewed by the owner to verify final calendar availability.
  • Booking Deposit: If approved, we will send you a secure Stripe link by email to pay a 30% deposit of the total amount. The booking is only confirmed once this deposit is received. You have 48 hours to complete this payment once the link is sent.
  • Remaining Balance: The remaining 70% must be paid at least 30 days before your arrival date, using the payment link provided or via bank transfer.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Free Cancellation: You can cancel your booking free of charge with a 100% refund of the amount paid up to 30 days before your arrival date.
  • Late Cancellation: If you cancel less than 30 days before the arrival date, payments made up to that date are non-refundable.

3. Tourist Tax (EcoTasa)

According to the regulations of the Balearic Islands Government, the Sustainable Tourism Tax (EcoTasa) applies. The rate is €2.20 per night per adult (aged 16 and over). This tax is not included in the villa's base rate and must be paid obligatorily in cash on the day of arrival at the accommodation.

4. Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in: Starting from 4:00 PM. Please inform us in advance of your estimated arrival time.
  • Check-out: Before 10:00 AM to allow for thorough cleaning and preparation before the next guests arrive.

5. House Rules

  • The maximum capacity of the property is 6 guests plus babies, distributed in 3 bedrooms and 5 beds. Exceeding this limit is not permitted.
  • Parties, social events, or loud gatherings that disrupt the neighborhood's peace are strictly prohibited.
  • Smoking is not allowed inside any of the indoor villa rooms.
  • Pets are allowed only upon prior request and explicit confirmation by the owner.

Enjoy your dream holiday in Mallorca in our traditional private villa with a pool, citrus gardens, and all modern comforts.
