Exclusive Services

Designed for Your Comfort & Enjoyment

We combine Mallorcan rustic charm with modern comforts to ensure you have everything you need for your holiday.

EV Charging Station

Dedicated integrated EV charger (Type 2 socket) to charge your electric vehicle directly at the property.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

Fast and wide-reaching fiber optic connection. Ideal for both streaming and remote working.

BBQ & Outdoor Kitchen

Fully equipped summer kitchen featuring a traditional brick charcoal barbecue next to the pool.

Ping-pong Table

Outdoor activity area with a table tennis table, paddles and balls provided for family fun.

Exclusive-use swimming pool surrounded by comfortable sun loungers, parasols and absolute privacy.

Family Friendly
