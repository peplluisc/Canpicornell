How to Get to Can Picornell

Detailed instructions and visual references to reach the villa without any trouble.

Official Address

Av. del Cid, 481, 07198 Palma, Balearic Islands, Spain
⚠️ Important Information about the access street:
The side street leading to the estate does not have an official house number. Therefore, you should set your GPS navigation system to go to Avenida del Cid, 481 (which marks the corner of the side entrance street).

Direct GPS Navigation

Click on the button corresponding to your device to launch GPS navigation to the entrance corner:

Open in Google Maps Open in Apple Maps

Visual Route Guide

Step 1: Identify the Corner

Go to Avenida del Cid, 481. When you reach the corner (you will see the corner house with number 481 and a green trash bin next to it), turn into the side street in the direction indicated by the red arrow.

Step 2: Continue to the Entrance

Continue straight along this street for approximately 220 meters. Just before reaching the end of the road, on your right-hand side, you will see the black metal gates to Can Picornell with its stone sign post.
